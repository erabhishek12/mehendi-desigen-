Meet the Artist

Hello, I'm Vedika

Welcome to VEDIKA MEHANDI DESIGN! With a passion for the timeless art of henna, I specialize in creating bespoke mehandi designs that tell a story. For me, every stroke is a celebration of tradition, beauty, and your unique personality.

From intricate bridal patterns to contemporary minimalist styles, I use only 100% natural and skin-safe henna to ensure a rich, dark stain and a delightful experience. Let's create something beautiful together for your next special occasion!
